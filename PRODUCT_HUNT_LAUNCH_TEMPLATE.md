## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Don't ask for upvotes directly** - Ask for visits and feedback instead
2. **Don't ignore comments** - Respond within 1 hour during launch day
3. **Don't launch on weekends** - Tuesday-Thursday are best
4. **Don't use company accounts** - Use personal maker accounts
5. **Don't spam communities** - Share authentically, not excessively
6. **Don't forget to prepare** - Start building community 2-3 weeks before
7. **Don't launch incomplete products** - Ensure core features work perfectly
