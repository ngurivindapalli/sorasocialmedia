## Content Themes & Topics

### Main Topics Covered:
1. **AI-Native Consulting Firms**
   - Rethinking value creation end-to-end
   - Moving beyond "sprinkling tools"
   - Reshaping consulting foundations
   - Blurring line between software, services, knowledge, and automation

2. **AI Reshaping Consulting Work**
   - Single senior operator + multiple AI agents model
   - Shifting pyramid structures
   - Expanding quality and throughput without increasing headcount
   - Consultant + agent teams as practical expansion model

3. **Responsible AI**
   - Governance, auditability, provenance
   - Cultural readiness for regulatory compliance
   - Operating discipline, not just policy
   - Underestimated by most firms

4. **AI Services Ecosystem**
   - Fastest-growing job category in America
   - Companies at various stages: drafting roadmaps, stuck in pilot purgatory, scaling
   - Need for strategists who are part technologist, part therapist for confused executives
